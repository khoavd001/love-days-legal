Love Dairy Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Love Dairy, also shown in the app as Love Days, is a private relationship

journal for couples. This policy explains what data the app collects, why it is

used, and the choices you have.

Data We Collect

When you use Love Dairy, we may collect:

  • Account data: email address, display name, authentication state, and account

identifiers.

  • Couple profile data: couple name, relationship start date, partner role,

invite codes, profile photo, cover photo, and app preferences.

  • Journal content: memories, diary entries, captions, comments, reactions,

moods, tags, milestones, reels, reminders, and notifications.

  • Media data: photos, videos, thumbnails, edited media, avatars, export files,

file metadata, and storage paths.

  • Location data: only when you add a place to a memory or explicitly allow

location access. This may include latitude, longitude, place name, address,

and related metadata.

  • Device permission data: camera, microphone, photo library, notifications,

and location permissions as needed for features you choose to use.

  • Technical data: app version, device/platform information, network errors,

authentication events, and security logs needed to operate and protect the

service.

  • Advertising data: advertising identifiers, device identifiers, approximate

ad interaction data, and consent status used to show and measure ads.

We do not intentionally collect payment data in the current app. We use Google

AdMob to show ads and measure ad performance. We do not sell personal data.

How We Use Data

We use data to:

  • Create and secure your account.
  • Sync your private couple space across your devices.
  • Show memories, diary entries, reels, maps, reminders, notifications, and

profile settings.

  • Let you invite a partner and manage shared couple content.
  • Save media to private storage buckets and generate signed access URLs.
  • Diagnose bugs, prevent abuse, enforce security rules, and improve app

reliability.

  • Comply with legal, store, and platform requirements.

Sharing And Processors

Your couple content is intended for you and your active partner, subject to the

visibility settings in the app. Private entries are restricted by backend access

rules.

We use service providers to run the app, including Supabase for authentication,

database, storage, realtime sync, and email verification. Apple, Google, and

your device operating system may process data when you install the app, approve

permissions, receive notifications, use deep links, or use platform services.

Google AdMob may process advertising identifiers, device information, and ad

interaction data to deliver, cap, personalize where allowed, and measure ads.

We may disclose data if required by law, to protect users, to investigate abuse,

or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or similar business transfer.

Permissions

Love Dairy asks for permissions only when a feature needs them:

  • Camera and microphone: capture photos or videos in the app.
  • Photos and videos: choose or save media.
  • Location: attach a place to a memory or show location-based memory features.
  • Notifications: deliver reminders and couple activity alerts.

You can change these permissions in your device settings. Some features may not

work without the related permission.

Security

Love Dairy uses authenticated Supabase sessions, row-level security, private

storage buckets, and signed media URLs to limit access to couple data. No

internet service can be guaranteed perfectly secure, so keep your account

credentials private and tell us if you suspect unauthorized access.

Retention And Deletion

We keep account, couple, journal, media, reminder, and notification data while

your account or couple space is active, unless a shorter period is required by

law or product behavior. Deleted or disabled content may remain in backups,

logs, or security records for a limited time before removal.

To request account-wide access, correction, export, or deletion, email

khoavd111+love.days.support@gmail.com or use the account deletion page linked

from the app store listing. Deletion requests may require account verification

before we remove account data.

Children

Love Dairy is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum digital consent

age in your country. Do not use the app if you are not old enough to consent to

these terms.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we

use appropriate safeguards for service providers and platform processors.

Changes

We may update this policy as Love Dairy changes. The updated version will show a

new "Last updated" date. Material changes may also be announced in the app or

through the relevant store listing.
