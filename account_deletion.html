Amoprite Account Deletion

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Amoprite lets users permanently delete their app account and associated data from inside the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open Amoprite and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Open Profile.
  3. In the Account section, tap Delete Account.
  4. Read the permanent-deletion notice and confirm. If an Apple confirmation sheet appears, complete it to verify the signed-in account.

Deletion begins from the app after confirmation. You do not need to send an email or wait for a support agent.

What can be deleted

  • Your authentication account, profile, settings, and active memberships
  • Diary entries, memories, comments, reactions, moods, milestones, reminders, and notifications you created
  • Photos, videos, thumbnails, reels, edited media, avatars, exports, and other media you uploaded
  • Location data attached to content you created

Shared couple data

If your couple has an active partner, their account is not deleted and they become the owner of the couple space. Shared or derived items that depend on content you delete may also be removed. If no active partner remains, the couple space and its remaining content are deleted.

Subscriptions

Deleting an Amoprite account does not automatically cancel an App Store or Google Play subscription. Cancel an active subscription in your device's store subscription settings.

What may be retained temporarily

Some deleted or disabled data may remain for a limited time in backups, logs, security records, or records required for legal, fraud prevention, or abuse prevention purposes.
