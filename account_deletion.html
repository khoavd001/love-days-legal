Love Days Account Deletion

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Love Days lets users request deletion of their app account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Email khoavd111+love.days.support@gmail.com from the email address used for your Love Days account. Use the subject "Love Days account deletion request".

Include your account email and a short note confirming that you want your Love Days account and associated app data deleted. We may ask you to verify account ownership before processing the request.

What can be deleted

  • Account and profile data
  • Couple profile data
  • Diary entries, memories, comments, reactions, moods, milestones, reminders, and notifications
  • Photos, videos, thumbnails, reels, edited media, avatars, and storage metadata
  • Location data attached to memories

What may be retained temporarily

Some deleted or disabled data may remain for a limited time in backups, logs, security records, or records required for legal, fraud prevention, or abuse prevention purposes.

Contact

For privacy questions, email khoavd111+love.days.support@gmail.com.
